How to Play

🍎🍌

Tap the Fruit

Click apples and bananas as fast as you can before they vanish. Each hit adds to your score.

✨

Golden Fruit

Golden apples & bananas are worth +10 points. Don't miss them!

💣

Avoid Bombs

Clicking a bomb costs you -5 points and shakes the screen. Steer clear!

🔥

Build Combos

Chain hits without missing to multiply your score up to 4x.

❤️

Guard Your Lives

Letting a fruit disappear costs a life. Lose all 3 and it's game over.

⭐

Grab Power-ups

Double Score, Slow Time, Freeze Timer, Magnet and Extra Life all help you survive longer.
